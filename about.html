Food

Manta rays eat tiny ocean creatures called plankton. They swim with their mouths open to filter these small particles from the water, almost like using a net.

Personality

Manta rays are gentle and calm animals. They are curious and often swim close to divers, and they are not dangerous.

Habitat

Manta rays live in warm ocean waters around the world. You can find them near coral reefs, along coastlines, and sometimes in the open sea.

Life Span

Manta rays can live a long time. Many live around 20 to 30 years, and some may reach 40 years or more.
