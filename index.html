What is Manta Ray?

Manta rays are the gentle giants of the sea, they are from a big family with sharks and other cool rays. There are two main kinds of manta, first is the super-big giant oceanic manta, it can grow up to 7 meter wide! Second is the friendlier reef manta, which is about half the size of giant manta, and they love to swim around the coral reef. The oceanic ones love wandering far for adventures, while reef mantas stick closer to home near islands and bays. Here is a fun fact about mantas: Each manta has its own special "belly and back fingerprints" formed with spots and patterns! I love manta ray because they are intelligent and peaceful animals!
